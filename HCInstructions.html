Materials

12 Volt Motor, Wire, Electrical Tape, 8 Spoons/Scoops, Bottle caps

Step 1: Preparing the Motor

Materials: Motor, Wire, Electrical Tape

  • Gather 2 Wires
  • Strip the ends of each wire
  • Secure exposed end of wire to the metal forks of the motor
  • Attach with electrical tape

Step 2: Preparing the Water Wheel

Materials: Spoons, Bottle Caps

  • Adjust length of spoons/scoops to liking
  • Layout 8 spoons equidistant from one another one a bottle cap and secure with adhesive
  • Lay another same size bottle cap on exposed side of spoons
  • Create a whole through one of the bottle caps identical in diameter to the motor shaft

Step 3: Assembling the Generator

Materials: Water Wheel, Assembled Motor

  • Insert motor shaft to hole created in the water wheel during step 2
  • Generator should be secure as is. If not, secure with adhesive
