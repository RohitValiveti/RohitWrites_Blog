About Me

Hi, I'm Rohit.

Burrito enthusiast, Traveler, Suburbanite.

Originally, I was just going to have images of my projects, but while working on it, I decided to have it be a tutotial-style maker blog where people (or myself) can replicate the projects in the future. To create the website, I used HTML/CSS.
