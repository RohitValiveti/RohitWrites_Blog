Marble Roller Coaster

This was probably one of my favorite projects to undertake. I got inspiration from several videos on the internet of people using generic templates, which ended up limited their possibilities. So, to make the track unique to me, I designed my own pieces. Some of the noteworthy components include an elevator, a vertical loop, and a funnel. One of my favorite aspects of this creation is that I can keep on adding to it due to the creation of an elevator, which resets the marble back to the top, and the design is modular, meaning smaller components make up the piece as a whole.

Hood Mask

I am probably most proud of this creation. The hood mask was designed and created by my friends and me. It is a mask made entirely out of shoelaces that attaches to the inside of a hood and protects the user from the cold while also serving as a convenient holder for a pollution mask. We submitted our creation to the Deca Idea challenge and were titled global winners out of over 5000 submissions! The video linked shows how to make the mask and it in use.
